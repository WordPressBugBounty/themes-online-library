== Description ==

Online Library is a modern, versatile, and fully responsive theme designed for bookshops, eBook stores, publishing houses, digital libraries, and reading platforms, making it ideal for content focused on writing, journal publications, and stories. Perfect for online bookstores, author showcases, academic libraries, eLearning hubs, and educational resource centers, this theme offers a clean and organized layout with easy navigation through categories, genres, and featured titles. It supports seamless integration with WooCommerce for selling books, eBooks, and learning materials, while Contact Form 7 enables smooth reader inquiries, subscriptions, and engagement. Designed with flexibility in mind, it allows full customization of colors, typography, and layouts to match your brand identity. Its SEO-friendly and performance-optimized structure improves search visibility and ensures fast loading, while the responsive design guarantees a seamless browsing experience across desktops, tablets, and smartphones. Online Library provides a professional, elegant, and conversion-focused platform for authors, publishers, educators, and book enthusiasts to showcase, manage, and sell collections centered around writing, journals, and storytelling.
